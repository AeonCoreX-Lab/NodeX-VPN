// How it works

Three hops.
Zero traces.

Each circuit uses three independent Tor relays. No single relay knows both who you are and what you're accessing.

💻
Your Device
🛡️
Guard Relay
🔄
Middle Relay
🌐
Exit Relay
🔒
Destination
01

Guard Relay

Knows your IP but not your destination. Uses a stable, trusted guard for long-term anonymity.

02

Middle Relay

Knows neither your origin nor destination. Prevents traffic correlation attacks between entry and exit.

03

Exit Relay

Knows the destination but not you. Choose any exit country — fully serverless, no servers of ours.

Built right.

🦀

Rust + Arti Engine
